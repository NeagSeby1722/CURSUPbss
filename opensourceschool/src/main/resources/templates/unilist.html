Universitatea Politehnica Timișoara

Since 1920

The Politehnica University of Timișoara (UPT) is a leading technical university in Romania, renowned for its engineering and technology programs. Founded in 1920, UPT is a center of innovation and practical learning, shaping future engineers and tech leaders. With a strong emphasis on research and industry partnerships, UPT drives progress in Timișoara and beyond.

Universitatea de Vest Timișoara

Since 1944

The West University of Timișoara (UVT) is a vibrant hub of learning and innovation in Romania, blending tradition with modern research across fields from arts to sciences. Known for its global outlook and dynamic campus life, UVT is where culture, creativity, and curiosity come together in the heart of Timișoara.
