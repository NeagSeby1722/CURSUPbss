About CursUP

CursUP is a platform created by students, for students. Our mission is to simplify the learning process by providing an easy-to-use platform where students can upload, share, and access study materials. We believe in the power of collaboration and aim to build a community where knowledge is freely shared to enhance academic success.

Our Mission

At CursUP, our mission is to empower students by providing a platform that fosters collaboration, accessibility, and innovation in education. We strive to break down barriers to learning and create a supportive environment where every student has the resources they need to succeed.
